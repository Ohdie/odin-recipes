Mugcake Recipe

This easy mug cake is a delightful dessert that comes together in minutes. With its rich chocolate flavor (if using cocoa),

it's a quick and satisfying treat perfect for when you're craving something sweet without the hassle of baking a whole cake. Enjoy it straight from the mug for minimal cleanup!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder (optional)
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract, and mix until smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute (adjust time based on your microwave's power).
  4. Let it cool slightly before enjoying!

    5. Enjoy the Mugcake!
