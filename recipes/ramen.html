Ramen Recipe

This quick ramen with mixed egg is a comforting dish that combines tender noodles with a rich, creamy egg. As the noodles cook, the egg is mixed in, creating a silky broth that enhances the flavor and texture. Perfect for a satisfying meal in just a few minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (500 ml) water
  • 1 package instant ramen (any flavor)
  • 1 large egg
  • Optional toppings: green onions, sesame seeds, or vegetables

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a pot.
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. In the last minute of cooking, crack the egg directly into the pot and stir gently to mix it in.
  4. Add the seasoning packet and stir well.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with optional toppings.

    6. Enjoy the Ramen!
