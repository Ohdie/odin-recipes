Sunny side up Egg Recipe

This sunny side up egg with bacon and cheese is a classic breakfast that combines rich flavors and textures.

The creamy melted cheese complements the crispy bacon and the luscious yolk, creating a perfect harmony on your plate. Enjoy it with toast or on its own for a hearty start to your day!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 strips of bacon
  • 1 slice of cheese (cheddar or your preference)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. In a frying pan, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  2. In the same pan, crack the eggs gently and cook until the whites are set but the yolks remain runny, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Place the cheese slice on top of the eggs and cover the pan briefly to melt the cheese.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve with bacon and optional herbs.

    5. Enjoy the Egg!
